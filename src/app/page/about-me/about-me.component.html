About me
I am Thien (David) Vuong Huu. A UX/UI Designer from Vietnam with over 7 years of experience, including 3 years as a Full-Stack Developer. My technical background in Angular 2+, .NET, and various UI frameworks such as Material UI, SCSS, Tailwind CSS, Prime NG, Kendo UI, Chakra UI, and Bootstrap allows me to create intuitive and highly functional, responsive designs. Over the past 4 years, I've specialized in UX/UI design, working with tools like Miro, Figma, and Illustrator. My dual experience bridges development and design, optimizing workflows and improving efficiency. For example, in an Australian government project (HBCF), I helped reduce workflow by over 20% by developing UX solutions, minimizing system errors, and reducing bug-fixing time. In addition, I also participate in supporting the Figma community. More than 16.000 designers are using my designs.
